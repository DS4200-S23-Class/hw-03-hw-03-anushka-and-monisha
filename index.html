The button has been clicked:

Marks

  • Electoral Vote Indicator: Areas
  • Map: Areas

Channels

  1. Color(hue)
    • Represents the two seperate 2016 candidate's, specifically their political party affiliation
    • This channel was a good idea because it color is an easily perceptible channel and this attribute of political party affiliation is a well-known one that is consistently identified by two distinct colors: red and blue. Also, it is a proper identity channel

Colormaps

  1. Categorical
    • Represents political party affiliation of each 2016 candidate
    • This colormap was a good idea because the attrubte being represented is categorical. The data does not have a range or difference in values on the same scales. With a nominal attribute like political parties, it is best represented with a categorical colormap.

Marks

  • Batted Balls: Points

Channels

  1. Color(hue) and Saturation
    • Represents if the scoring value of batted balls was higher or lower than average LWTS
    • This channel was a good idea, as colors and their intensities are a good way to demonstrate magnitudes.
    • The Color(hue) channel is an Identity, categorical attribute, where as the color saturation is a Magniture, ordered attribute
  2. Spatial Region
    • Represents the type of ball the batted ball is based on area on the chart
    • This channel is a good idea as it the area on the map clearly shows the different categories of balls that were batted

Colormaps

  1. Divergent
    • Represents the magnitude of scoring values of batted balls based on saturation and hue
    • This colormap made it a bit difficult to intuitivey identify below/above average balls. Instead of orange and purple, using red and green would have been a better choice in my opinion.

Marks

  • Each individual flower: points

Channels

  1. Color(hue)
    • Represents the different species of flower
    • This channel was a good idea because color is an easily perceptible channel and can clearly show the different categories(species) of the flowers.
    • This channel is an Identity, categorical attribute

Colormaps

  1. Categorical
    • Represents the specific species of flower
    • This colormap was a good idea because the colors are distinct enough that people won't get confused as to which color corresponds to each species. There also isn't a need for an ordering, so not using any of those channel attributes was a good choice.
